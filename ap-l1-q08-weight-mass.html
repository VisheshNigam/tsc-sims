Q8 Forces — Weight vs Mass L1 · Conceptual
An astronaut has a mass of 70 kg on Earth. What happens to their mass and weight on the Moon? (Moon's gravity ≈ 1/6 of Earth's)
Mass is the amount of matter — it never changes. Weight = mg, so it changes with gravity.
✓ Correct answer
💡 Did you know?
In deep space, far from any planet, your weight is essentially zero — but your mass (and therefore inertia) remains exactly the same!
