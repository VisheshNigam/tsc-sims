Q10 Energy — Work L1 · Conceptual
A force of 10 N pushes a box 5 m in the direction of the force. How much work is done on the box?
Work = Force × displacement (when force and displacement are parallel).
✓ Correct answer
💡 Did you know?
When you do work on an object, you transfer energy to it — 50 J of work done against friction becomes 50 J of thermal energy!
