Q2 Laws of Motion L2 · Numerical
A block of mass 2 kg on a rough horizontal surface (μk = 0.3) is pushed with a horizontal force F and accelerates at 2 m/s². Find F.  (g = 10 m/s²)
✓ Correct Answer
C — 10 N
💡 Don't Fall For This
Ignoring friction (→ 4 N): Applying F = ma directly gives 2 × 2 = 4 N — but this ignores friction entirely. The applied force must overcome friction and provide acceleration.

Selecting friction as the answer (→ 6 N): Calculating fk = μmg = 6 N and stopping there is a very common trap. That is only the friction force — you still need additional force to produce the 2 m/s² acceleration.
