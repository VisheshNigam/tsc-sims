Q7 Kinematics — Free Fall L1 · Conceptual
A ball is dropped from rest. Ignoring air resistance, what is its speed after 3 seconds? (use g = 10 m/s²)
Use v = u + at, where initial speed u = 0 and a = g = 10 m/s².
✓ Correct answer

Galileo showed all objects fall at the same rate regardless of mass. On the Moon (no air), a feather and a hammer land together!
