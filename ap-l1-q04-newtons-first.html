Q4 Newton's First Law L1 · Conceptual
A hockey puck slides on a frictionless ice surface with no horizontal forces acting on it. What does it do?
Newton's First Law: with no net force, an object's state of motion does not change.
✓ Correct answer
💡 Did you know?
Newton's First Law is called the Law of Inertia. Inertia is why you lurch forward when a car brakes suddenly!
