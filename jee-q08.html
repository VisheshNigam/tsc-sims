Q8 Ray Optics L1 · Numerical
A convex lens of focal length 20 cm has an object 60 cm in front of it. The image distance is:
✓ Correct Answer
B — 30 cm
💡 Don't Fall For This
Sign convention error: The most common mistake is using u = +60 instead of u = −60. With u = +60, the formula gives 1/v = 1/20 − 1/60 = 2/60, leading to the wrong answer. Always assign a negative sign to real object distances in the New Cartesian convention.
