Q2 Kinematics — Velocity L1 · Conceptual
A car travels around a circular track at constant speed. Which statement about its velocity is correct?
Velocity is a vector — it has both magnitude AND direction.
✓ Correct answer
💡 Did you know?
A car moving in a circle at constant speed is always accelerating — because the direction of velocity keeps changing!
