Q9 Thermodynamics L2 · Conceptual
An ideal gas undergoes an isothermal expansion. Which statement is correct?
✓ Correct Answer
B — Work done by the gas is positive and heat is absorbed
💡 Don't Fall For This
Confusing isothermal with adiabatic (→ C): Isothermal means constant temperature (ΔT = 0) — heat CAN still be exchanged. Adiabatic means no heat exchange (Q = 0) — but temperature changes. These two are opposites and are the most commonly confused processes in thermodynamics.
