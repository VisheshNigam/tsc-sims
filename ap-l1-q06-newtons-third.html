Q6 Newton's Third Law L1 · Conceptual
A swimmer pushes backward on the water with 80 N of force. What force does the water exert on the swimmer?
Newton's Third Law: every action force has an equal and opposite reaction force.
✓ Correct answer
💡 Did you know?
Every force in nature comes in a pair — you can never push on something without it pushing back just as hard!
