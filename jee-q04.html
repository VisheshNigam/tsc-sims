Q4 Momentum & Collisions L1 · Numerical
Two bodies of 3 kg each collide perfectly inelastically. One moves at 6 m/s, the other is at rest. The final velocity is:
✓ Correct Answer
B — 3 m/s
💡 Don't Fall For This
Forgetting to add the masses (→ 6 m/s): Dividing momentum by just one mass (18 ÷ 3 = 6) ignores that both masses move together after impact. The combined mass is 3 + 3 = 6 kg, giving vf = 18 ÷ 6 = 3 m/s.
