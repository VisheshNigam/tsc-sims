Q3 Kinematics — Acceleration L1 · Conceptual
A ball rolling to the right slows down. What is the direction of its acceleration?
Acceleration points in the direction of the change in velocity — not necessarily the direction of motion.
✓ Correct answer
💡 Did you know?
'Deceleration' in physics just means acceleration pointing opposite to velocity — there's no separate quantity for it!
