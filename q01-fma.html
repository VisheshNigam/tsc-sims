Question 1
A 4 kg box accelerates at 3 m/s² across a frictionless surface. What is the net force acting on the box?
▶ See the Physics — F = ma Explorer
🔒
Answer to unlock simulation
Mass
4 kg
Net Force
12 N
Acceleration = F ÷ m
12 N ÷ 4 kg
3.0 m/s²
