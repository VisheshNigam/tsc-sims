Q7 Photoelectric Effect L1 · Numerical
The work function of a metal is 2 eV. The threshold frequency for photoelectric emission is approximately:
✓ Correct Answer
B — 4.8 × 10¹⁴ Hz
💡 Don't Fall For This
Skipping unit conversion: Plugging φ = 2 directly into ν₀ = φ/h without converting to Joules gives a wildly wrong answer. Always convert first: 2 eV × 1.6×10⁻¹⁹ = 3.2×10⁻¹⁹ J — then divide by h.
