Q6 Current Electricity L2 · Numerical
Three resistors of 12 Ω, 6 Ω, and 4 Ω are connected in parallel. The equivalent resistance is:
✓ Correct Answer
B — 2 Ω
💡 Don't Fall For This
Forgetting the final flip (→ 0.5 Ω): Getting 1/Req = 0.5 is correct — but stopping there gives you the reciprocal, not Req. Always invert: Req = 1/0.5 = 2 Ω.

Direct addition (→ 22 Ω): Adding 12 + 6 + 4 = 22 Ω is the series formula — not parallel. Parallel always gives a result smaller than the smallest individual resistor.
