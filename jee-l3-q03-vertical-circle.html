Q3 Circular Motion L3 · Numerical
A particle of mass m on a string of length L is rotated in a vertical circle. The minimum speed at the top to maintain a taut string is:
✓ Correct Answer
A — √(gL)
💡 Don't Fall For This
Confusing top speed with bottom speed (→ √(5gL)): √(5gL) is the minimum speed required at the bottom of the circle to complete the full loop — a completely different condition. At the top, only √(gL) is needed. This is the single most common error on this topic.
